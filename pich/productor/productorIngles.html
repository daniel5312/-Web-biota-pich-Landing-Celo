The Critical Barrier for Small Farmers: Getting Started

Small and medium producers face crippling challenges that prevent the transition to profitable regenerative farming:

  • **Financial Lockout:** Zero access to formal bank credit for new, sustainable projects.
  • **Soaring Input Costs:** Reliance on expensive chemical inputs makes traditional farming unsustainable.
  • **Dead Soil:** Loss of fertility means low yields and high risk.
  • **Generational Exodus:** Youth see no modern career path in their family's land.

The First Step: Inclusive Seed Capital (ReFi)

We focus on removing the highest hurdle—the initial financial investment and technical design—to ensure a successful transition to regenerative practices.

Micro-Financing Access

Immediate micro-loans and seed capital for the transition, bypassing traditional banking requirements.

Regenerative Farm Design

Expert diagnostic and tailored transition plan to rapidly restore soil health and biodiversity in their initial plot.

Transparent Blockchain Backing

The entire loan process and fund management is secured by transparent, user-friendly blockchain technology (invisible to the farmer).

Basic Training & Bio-Inputs

On-site training and resources to produce their own bio-inputs, drastically reducing their dependency on external costs.

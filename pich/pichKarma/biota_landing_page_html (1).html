Description

Biota is a Web3 ReFi platform that drives regenerative agriculture through on-chain traceability, incentives, education and payments in cCOP.

Mission

Accelerate the regenerative transition by empowering farmers with simple tools, fair markets and regenerative financing.

Problem

Farmers lack financing, technical assistance, reliable traceability and fair markets to adopt regenerative practices.

Solution

Modular Web3 ecosystem that combines DeFi, traceability, education and marketplace to reduce risks and accelerate the transition.

Business Model

Consulting, certifications, traceability, climate services, marketplace, and value generated by Web3 and ReFi modules.

Stage

Initial stage / Pre-MVP with pilots in Envigado and data collection to validate the model and prepare the MVP.

Funding

No external funding yet; self-funded while applying to grants and ReFi programs to scale.

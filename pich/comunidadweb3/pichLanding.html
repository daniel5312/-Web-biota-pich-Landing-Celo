Modular Ecosystem

Biota is built as independent modules so each milestone can be shipped as a mini‑app, claimed on Proof‑of‑Ship and used to show real progress.

BiotaPass

Dynamic NFT identity for producers and lots. Records evidence, POAPs and MRV data on Celo.

Traceability

On‑chain lot registration, geolocated photos, soil data and history for each harvest.

Marketplace

Fair‑price ecommerce with cCOP payments, direct payouts and minimal fees.

DeFi · ReFi

Regenerative micro‑finance, staking rewards and Divvi incentives to fund transitions.

Biofactory

Local production of inoculants, compost and biological inputs with QR traceability.

Academy

Workshops, certifications and on‑chain POAPs to show training and capacity building.

Impact & Roadmap

We focus on measurable environmental and social outcomes. Our pilot in Envigado gathers the data to validate the model and produce Biota Impact Units for future tokenization.

Short term: Deploy BiotaPass for pilot producer, register first lots, and run training sessions.
Mid term: Launch traceability mini‑app, marketplace beta, and biofactory pilot.
Long term: Validate carbon capture, tokenize impact units and scale regionally.
